Inter-connected High School is a new generation school

We have redesigned the education system to better fit the new technologies' and the current expectations of the professional world.
We want some kind of partnership with our students. Team spirit is the key in Inter-connected High School.

Interaction professionals/teachers/students

It's about opening up to the professional world in parallel with general studies through partnerships and the study of particular cases.

Advanced technology

Priority is given to the use of digital tools to keep pace with current technological advances.

Worldwide

Throughout the program, we open the borders by developing exchanges between our students and teachers around the world through videoconferencing.

Oral fluency

Through theatrical and entertainment activities.
We want our students to feel self-confident in front of an audience.
